Upgrading to the BitBox02

Since March 25th, 2016 the BitBox01 hardware wallet has helped protect your funds from a potentially compromised computer. We pioneered microSD card backups and key ring portability.

Four years on, the market has matured. We decided to stop selling the BitBox01 with firmware updates phased out by November 2020. More information about the phase out is available in our FAQs.

We believe that the BitBox02 is a better product to deal with existing and future threats. To learn more, see our threat model and detailed BitBox02 security features.
